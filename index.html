The PCMP (Preventive Care Management Program) is a bolt-on wellness and benefits plan that plugs into your existing payroll — fully funded by federal tax incentives, not new budget.

For Your Business

  • $620+ average payroll tax savings per enrolled W-2 employee each year
  • Often up to 3× that value with lower premiums and fewer avoidable claims
  • Entirely funded through federal tax incentives — no new budget required
  • Free ACA compliance reporting (1094 & 1095s) — saves on filing fees
  • Better benefits package to attract higher-quality talent
  • Potential premium reduction on primary health insurance at renewal

For Your Employees

  • Zero cost to employees — new benefits added on top of what they already have
  • Savings up to $3,600 a year on wellness, copays and everyday care
  • Net take-home pay stays the same while access to care improves
  • AI-powered support & 24/7 telemedicine for employees and families
  • Primary & urgent care with no copays or deductibles
  • Mental health support, zero-cost prescriptions & free gym membership
